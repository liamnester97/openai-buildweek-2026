# Version 2.0 Revision Notes

## Added project idea reviewed

The project note proposed walking through a dinosaur exhibit at night, entering exhibit artwork or reconstructed scenes, becoming the represented dinosaur, exploring its natural habitat, and completing mini-games.

## Approved interpretation

- The museum is now an explorable nighttime hub.
- Exhibits, murals, dioramas, reconstructed scenes, and displays can act as portals.
- The visitor transforms into the featured prehistoric creature inside a portal.
- The Build Week MVP contains one complete portal rather than many incomplete exhibits.
- The first portal is **The Floodplain Memory**, with the player becoming a young Stegosaurus.
- Portal mini-games produce evidence-bearing memory fragments that are verified in the museum.
- The original Allosaurus–Stegosaurus scientific mystery remains the narrative spine.

## Major plan changes

- Rewritten product charter, story, game loop, MVP, success metrics, and roadmap.
- New museum hub, portal transition, transformation, habitat zones, mini-games, and return flow.
- Full-body Stegosaurus sprite atlas, portal effects, parallax habitat, HUD, audio, and fallback requirements.
- React museum shell plus a client-only Phaser habitat canvas.
- Shared deterministic TypeScript domain remains authoritative across both modes.
- New commands, events, API routes, portal checkpoints, persistence records, and failure recovery.
- Expanded Codex `/goal` stages, subagent roles, review gates, test suites, playtests, deployment, and demo plan.
